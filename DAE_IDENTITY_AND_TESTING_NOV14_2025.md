## üîç Diagnostic Questions

After running the test, assess:

### 1. Emission Quality

**Question:** Do emissions demonstrate DAE understood the user's story?

**Good signs:**
- Mirrors specific themes from story (burnout, scapegoating, safety, etc.)
- Names felt states accurately ("overwhelm," "carrying weight," "exhale")
- Appropriate somatic acknowledgment ("clenched jaw," "tight chest")

**Bad signs:**
- Generic responses that could apply to any story
- Misses core themes entirely
- No body/process acknowledgment

---

### 2. Family Clustering

**Question:** Do similar stories get similar nexuses?

**Good signs:**
- Both burnout stories trigger crisis/urgency nexuses
- Both psychological_safety stories trigger trust/healing nexuses
- Common nexuses across stories in same expected family

**Bad signs:**
- Random nexus detection with no pattern
- Same story gets different nexuses on re-run
- No thematic clustering

---

### 3. Confidence Patterns

**Question:** Is organism confident when it should be?

**Good signs:**
- High confidence for clear themes (burnout, scapegoating)
- Lower confidence for ambiguous/complex stories (parts conflict)
- Confidence correlates with emission quality

**Bad signs:**
- Uniformly low confidence across all stories
- High confidence but generic/off-topic emissions
- Confidence doesn't correlate with V0 descent
