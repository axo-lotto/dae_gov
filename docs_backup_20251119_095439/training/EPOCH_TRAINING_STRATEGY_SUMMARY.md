## Risk Assessment and Mitigation

### High Priority Risks (Mitigate Before Training)

**Risk 1: Single Family Dominance**
- **Diagnosis:** All conversations ‚Üí Family_001, no diversity
- **Impact:** Prevents generalization, defeats organic learning
- **Mitigation:** Lower similarity threshold (0.75 ‚Üí 0.65), increase variance weighting, validate ‚â•5 families by epoch 5

**Risk 2: Tau Threshold Too Strict**
- **Diagnosis:** COMMITTED regime (tau=0.75) rejects all emissions
- **Impact:** Organism stops emitting, no learning
- **Mitigation:** Adaptive tau (lower if success rate < 50%), monitor per-regime success rates

**Risk 3: Overfitting to Training Corpus**
- **Diagnosis:** High in-distribution, poor transfer
- **Impact:** Memorization, not intelligence
- **Mitigation:** Reserve 20% held-out test set, run transfer tests every 3 epochs, add noise/paraphrasing

### Medium Priority Risks (Monitor During Training)

**Risk 4: R-Matrix Saturation**
- **Mitigation:** Saturation term (1 - R[i,j]) prevents runaway, monitor mean < 0.70, std > 0.15

**Risk 5: Processing Time Explosion**
- **Mitigation:** Max iterations cap (5), early convergence termination, profile if time > 1s

**Risk 6: Kairos Never Detected**
- **Current:** 0% Kairos rate (window too narrow)
- **Mitigation:** Widen window (0.45-0.70 ‚Üí 0.35-0.75), target 20-40% by epoch 10
