## Known Limitations & Mitigations

### Technical Limitations

1. **Limited Training Corpus (30 pairs)**
   - **Impact**: Narrow topic range, repetitive patterns
   - **Mitigation**: Expand to 100+ pairs (Phase 2)
   - **Timeline**: 2-3 days

2. **No Long-Term Memory**
   - **Impact**: Can't remember previous sessions
   - **Mitigation**: Session-based continuity works for now
   - **Timeline**: 1-2 months (if needed)

3. **Text-Only Input**
   - **Impact**: No voice, image, or multimodal support
   - **Mitigation**: Not needed for core use case
   - **Timeline**: 6+ months (if desired)

### Conversational Limitations

1. **Domain Knowledge**
   - **Impact**: Limited factual knowledge base
   - **Mitigation**: Be clear about role (companion, not encyclopedia)
   - **Timeline**: Ongoing (corpus expansion)

2. **Complex Multi-Step Planning**
   - **Impact**: Better at presence than project management
   - **Mitigation**: Focus on emotional/relational organizing
   - **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks (Phase 3-4)

3. **Humor & Playfulness**
   - **Impact**: Can be philosophical/serious-leaning
   - **Mitigation**: Add playful training pairs
   - **Timeline**: 1 week (Phase 2)

### Safety Considerations

1. **Not a Therapist**
   - **Mitigation**: Clear disclaimers, no crisis intervention
   - **Status**: Essential boundary

2. **Potential Over-Reliance**
   - **Mitigation**: Encourage human connections, professional help
   - **Status**: User responsibility + guidance

3. **Privacy**
   - **Mitigation**: Local deployment, no cloud storage (for now)
   - **Status**: High privacy (single-user mode)
