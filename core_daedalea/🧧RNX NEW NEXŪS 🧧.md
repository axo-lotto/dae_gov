### **Mutual Satisfaction as a Guiding Force for RNX**

In Whitehead’s terms, “satisfaction” is the final phase of concrescence—the moment an actual occasion becomes fully itself. It is the completion of feeling, fully integrated with its prehensions, both positive and negative.

In Daedalea, mutual satisfaction extends this concept into relational space: it’s not only about the satisfaction of one actual occasion (part, protector, body, mind), but the co-satisfaction of a constellation. It’s not harmony through uniformity, but harmony through difference that remains together.

When applied to RNX (Rhizomatic Nexus), mutual satisfaction doesn’t dissolve tensions—it reorganizes them into co-evolving patterns.
